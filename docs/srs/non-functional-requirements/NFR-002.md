# NFR-002: BLE Reconnect Reliability

## Requirement

**Category:** Reliability
**Statement:** After a controller link dropout while the controller is paired in NVS, the firmware shall successfully reconnect within **5 s** in at least **90%** of dropout events, under nominal RF conditions (≤ 3 m line-of-sight, no active 2.4 GHz interferers in the immediate vicinity).

**Priority:** Must Have
**Status:** Draft

## Traceability

| Traces To | ID | Description |
|-----------|-----|-------------|
| Customer Need | CN.5 | Establish and re-establish a BLE-HID connection automatically |
| Applies To FRs | FR-008 | Auto-reconnect |
| Customer Problem | CP.4 | Maintain a reliable wireless link to the Xbox controller |

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] 10-trial dropout benchmark: ≥ 9 reconnects complete within 5 s
- [ ] No trial requires a reboot, button press on the controller, or operator action other than ensuring the controller is powered on
- [ ] Median reconnect time across the 10 trials ≤ 2 s

## Measurement Method

Scripted test: place controller in a controlled location, power-cycle the controller via a remote-controlled relay or USB switch, log reconnect timestamps from the firmware's serial telemetry.
