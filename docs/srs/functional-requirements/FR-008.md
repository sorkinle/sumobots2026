# FR-008: Auto-Reconnect

## Requirement

**Statement:** After a controller-link dropout while a controller BLE address is stored in NVS, the firmware shall automatically and continuously attempt to reconnect to that controller, without requiring operator intervention, button presses on the controller, or a firmware reboot.

**Priority:** Must Have
**Status:** Draft

## Traceability

| Traces To | ID | Description |
|-----------|-----|-------------|
| Customer Need | CN.5 | Establish and re-establish a BLE-HID connection automatically |
| Customer Problem | CP.4 | The robot must maintain a reliable wireless link to the Xbox controller |

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] After a dropout, reconnect attempts begin within 1 s
- [ ] Under nominal RF conditions (≤ 3 m line-of-sight, no active 2.4 GHz interferers), reconnect succeeds within 5 s in ≥ 90% of 10 trials
- [ ] Reconnect happens with no operator action other than ensuring the controller is powered on
- [ ] On successful reconnect, the State Machine resumes from DISARMED (never directly ARMED)

## Notes

NFR-002 pins down the reliability budget (90% within 5 s). FR-008 is the behavior requirement.
